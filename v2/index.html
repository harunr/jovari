Exceptionally yours.

Every Jovari RCC1 begins with a performance-tuned carbon-fiber monocoque with a race car pedigree, transformed into an ultra-luxury GT.completely customized to its owner,

by the world’s top artisans using the most-exotic woods, and finest gems, hybrid and natural hides, all in the colours and even patterns of your choosing.

Opening new directions

Our patented and patent-pending inventions ensure your Jovari is years ahead of other vehicles.

These include patented (pending) doors which open automatically to the side of the vehicle, and even extend an umbrella in the rain. Smart sensors ensure safety, and even detect you and your passengers as you approach.

Expanding practicality

Our patented and patent-pending adventure rack extends at the press of a button (or a voice command) to hold two bicycles of any size or style, or an array of compatible accesories including a cooler and BBQ grille for your next journey, picknick, or tailgate.

Hard headed but open minded

Our patent-pending hardtop keeps you safe from the elements, but extends swiftly to convert your Jovari into an open road paradise.

When closed, the roof is strong enough to hold a storage container or additional rack, even a tent.

The design process

You will sit with our designers and artists in our studio or at your location, and work with our team to ensure your Jovari tells your unique story.

First, we capture the theme and story you wish your Jovari to tell.

As you speak, our international team of designers and artists sketch and render possibilities, reviewing colours, materials, engravings, marquetry, inlays, stitching, and even customising digital interface.

Production

Once your specification (”spec”) is finalized, our international team of crafstmen, engineers, sourcers, and artists will begin to fabricate your custom, one-of-a-kind Jovari from the world’s finest materials.

Parts are made by hand, but also by the latest in CNC machinery and laser cutters to achieve perfect tolerances.

Every oart goes through a throrough review and measurement process and is photographed and recorder for archival and repair purposes.

Testing & support

Jovari vehicles go through extensive testing, from safety and impact tests, to electronics, materials, weather, and performance.

No vehicle leaves our factory without every centimeter being reviewed by our trained team, and every system and mechanism being throughly tested.

Should something go wrong, our network or exotic vehicle dealerships as well as our dedicated support team are here to assist.
